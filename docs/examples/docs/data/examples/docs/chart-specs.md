# Dashboard Chart Specifications

## 1) Outstanding Balance Overview
- Chart: KPI + total outstanding KES
- Purpose: show macro exposure

---

## 2) Aging Distribution
- Chart: Bar chart
- X-axis: Aging bucket
- Y-axis: Outstanding KES
- Purpose: payment delay severity

---

## 3) Department Breakdown
- Chart: Horizontal bar
- Show outstanding by department

---

## 4) Supplier Concentration
- Chart: Top 10 suppliers by outstanding balance

---

## 5) Payment Performance Trend
- Chart: Line chart
- Metric: average days to payment over time
