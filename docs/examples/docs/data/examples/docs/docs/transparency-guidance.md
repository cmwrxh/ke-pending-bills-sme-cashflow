# Transparency & Public Reporting Guidance

Pending bills transparency builds trust and improves SME liquidity.

---

## 1) Minimum disclosure dataset
- Department / agency
- Invoice amount
- Aging bucket
- Payment status

---

## 2) Privacy balance
- Avoid publishing supplier personal data
- Use aggregated reporting when necessary

---

## 3) Reporting frequency
- Monthly public summaries recommended
- Quarterly audit summaries

---

## 4) Public dashboard principles
- Clear totals
- Aging visualization
- Department accountability view

---

## 5) Risk awareness
- Misinterpretation of partial data
- Supplier confidentiality concerns
